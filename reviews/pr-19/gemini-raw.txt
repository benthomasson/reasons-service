I have completed the review of the authentication and RBAC implementation.

While the authentication logic itself (Google OAuth, Bearer tokens, and dev-mode bypass) is soundly implemented and well-tested, the **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) system is non-functional in practice**. The granular permissions defined in `rbac.py` are never applied to any routes, allowing any authenticated user—regardless of their assigned role—to perform any action, including deleting projects or managing beliefs.
